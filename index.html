Ghost from Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2

Simon "Ghost" Riley is one of the most iconic characters in the Call of Duty: Modern Warfare series. He is known for his mysterious appearance with a skull-patterned balaclava and a tactical headset. Ghost is a key member of Task Force 141, and his tactical skills and stealthy approach make him a lethal force on the battlefield.

Despite his mysterious nature, Ghost's loyalty and dedication to his team are unquestionable, and he plays a crucial role in the fight against global threats in the Modern Warfare series.

Modern Warfare 2, released in 2009, further cemented Ghost's status as a fan-favorite character, and he remains an integral part of the Call of Duty gaming community.

Task Force 141 is a multinational special operations unit composed of members of the British SAS, the United States Army Rangers, United States Navy SEALs, and the United States Air Force Special Operations Command. Task Force 141 appears in Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 and Call of Duty: Strike Team. The task force 141 includes the following oporators: Simon "ghost" Riley, Caption John Price and Sergeant Johnny MacTavish aka Soap.
